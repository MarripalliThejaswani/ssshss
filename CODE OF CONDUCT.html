Code of Conduct

Guidelines for disciplined living and character development in our hostel

Hostel Rules & Regulations

Essential guidelines for maintaining discipline and harmony

Humility is the hallmark of a good student

- Sri Sathya Sai Baba

Behavior & Respect

  • Practice obedience and reverence to teachers and elders
  • Maintain mutual courtesy and gentlemanly behavior
  • Promote peace and harmony among fellow students
  • Voluntarily maintain silence during assemblies, in dining hall, classrooms, and Mandir
  • Avoid loud talk and idle chatter in common areas

Personal Grooming

  • Wear decent and unostentatious dress at all times
  • Maintain short and well-groomed hairstyle
  • No moustache, beard, or long sideburns permitted
  • Maintain high standards of personal cleanliness
  • Keep living spaces and surroundings clean and tidy

Discipline & Punctuality

  • Punctuality is mandatory for all activities
  • Be on time for all school and hostel sessions
  • Follow the daily schedule without deviation
  • Write letters to parents at least once a week
  • Maintain silence during designated study hours

Hostel Premises

  • Do not leave hostel without explicit permission from warden
  • Overnight stays outside hostel are strictly forbidden
  • Parents may visit only between 10am-1pm on Sundays
  • No visitors permitted on working days
  • Follow proper procedures for emergency leave requests

Prohibited Items

  • No personal iron boxes or electrical appliances
  • No tape recorders, transistors, or electronic devices
  • No video games or entertainment devices
  • Avoid keeping ready cash or valuables
  • Only essential items permitted in rooms

Leave Policy

  • For essential leave, submit written application to warden
  • Include requisition letter from parents
  • Provide permission from school authorities
  • Medical leave requires doctor's approval
  • Return to hostel within stipulated time

Detailed Regulations

Specific guidelines for hostel residents

Category Regulation
Dress Code Simple, modest clothing in prescribed school colors
Hair Standards Short, neat haircuts (above collar for boys), no facial hair
Silence Periods Mandatory during prayers, meals, study hours and after lights out
Visiting Hours Sundays only (10am-1pm), parents/guardians only
Leave Requirements Parent letter + school permission + warden approval
Prohibited Items Electronics, valuables, cash over ₹500, personal appliances
Cleanliness Daily room inspection, weekly deep cleaning schedule
Communication Weekly letters home, no personal mobile phones

Commitment to Excellence

By following these guidelines, students develop discipline, character and spiritual growth
